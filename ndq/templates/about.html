News Done Quick (NDQ) was created because the Salty Software team is too lazy to read actual articles. This handy little tool lets you sign up, then sends you a link to a personalized feed of summarized news articles from a variety of reputable sources. Or, if you don't feel like signing up, just browse the summarized articles (updated every 15 minutes) from the main page. Created with love by Salty Software
